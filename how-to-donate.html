How to Donate

Here you'll find instructions on how to donate to the event

Giving Online (Preferred Option)

  1. Donate online by clicking here.
  2. Fill in Hack for the Specific Program / Activity field. Do not check the “Greatest needs of the college” box.
  3. Leave a comment if you would like and we’ll post it to our sponsor page on the website.
  4. Fill in the remainder of the fields with your information.
  5. Fill in the amount of money you would like to donate in the “Gift Amount” box at the bottom of the page.
  6. When asked to “Select Method of Payment”, click the continue button.
  7. Enter your card information and click the continue button.
  8. Review your payment option and click “Submit Gift”.
  9. We will receive email notifcation within 1 week of your donation, and then we will post you to our site.

By Check

  1. Please make the check payable to Potsdam College Foundation with the memo of Hack.
  2. Send the check to Potsdam College Foundation, Inc. at 44 Pierrepont Ave., Potsdam, NY 13676-2294, Raymond Hall 507.
  3. Email us your comment you'd like to leave for the event and we'll post you to the site!

Need Help?

If you need help making your gift, please contact Vicky Perry in the Foundation office at 315-267-3054. General questions about supporting Hack Potsdam should be directed to Jesse at peplinjj199@potsdam.edu
